Our Story

A quarter century of cardboard.

DP Sports Cards has been a trusted name in the vintage baseball card hobby since 1999. Known on Whatnot as @polakoff, this is one of the most-followed vintage baseball card shows on the platform — with nearly 8,000 followers and a community of serious collectors who show up week after week.

The specialty is pre-war, golden age, and vintage baseball cards — 1940s Bowman, 1950s and 1960s Topps, Hall of Fame rookies, and raw vintage lots that tell the story of America's pastime. Every card is described honestly. Every show feels like digging through a great collection.

Based in Orlando, FL, DP Sports Cards also buys and consigns collections locally. Whether you're across the country watching a live show or around the corner with cards to sell, you'll get a straight deal.
